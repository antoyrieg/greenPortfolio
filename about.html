I was born and raised in Detroit, MI. I have a passion for this community and the people in it - including the bad parts. Aside from my city, I also love music, spending time with family and friends. I also love good food.

Just as Mitch Albom once said,

Detroit is a place where we've had it pretty tough. But there is a generosity here and a well of kindness that goes deep.

People misunderstand Detroit and it's history. Because of that, the city gets written off as a place with not much hope or much a future. My goal not so much to change people's mindset, but to help Detroit shine a light (like it once did), that impacts and inspires cities all around the world.
